STEP 4 — BY Publication
=======================

Create ONE sub-folder per publication inside THIS folder, and place each
campaign Excel file inside its publication's sub-folder.

Example layout:

  04 Hubspot Email List - By Publication/
  |
  +-- CIO Quarterly Review/
  |       file.xlsx
  |
  +-- China Bi-Weekly/
          CN/
              file.xlsx
          EN/
              file.xlsx

Rules:
  - One sub-folder = one publication.
  - CN / EN language variants go inside CN / EN sub-folders.
  - Do NOT place Excel files loose in the root of this folder.

Then run Step 4 in the web app. It produces:
  Output/04/SG Hubspot Email List - BY Publication.xlsx
  Output/04/Master Panel SG Hubspot Email - BY Publication.xlsx  <-- the mapping file

(You can delete this text file.)
