One thing before the last step

Step 4 creates the Master Panel mapping file. Open its Campaign Mapping tab and, if needed, adjust two columns before running Step 5:

  • MUTE OR NOT — 1 hides a campaign completely; blank/0 keeps it.
  • Into All Publications or not? — 1/blank counts it everywhere; 0 keeps it only in its own publication.

Bonus tool

🧩 HubSpot Bulk Recipient Export

Adds a “Download selected (ZIP)” button to Hubspot so you can export many recipient lists at once.

⬇ Download (.zip)
How to install it in Chrome (4 steps)
  1. Unzip the download to a permanent folder (don’t delete it later).
  2. Open chrome://extensions and turn on Developer mode (top-right).
  3. Click Load unpacked and choose the unzipped folder (the one with manifest.json).
  4. In Hubspot → Marketing ▸ Email, tick emails and click Download selected (ZIP).

To share with a colleague, just send them the ZIP and these 4 steps.
